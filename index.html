Vicsek Model Simulation

Particle swarming simulation made with JavaScript and WebGL. Particles tend to follow the average direction of their nearby neighbours but fluctuate randomly depending on the level of noise. More information about the algorithm can be found here. See on CodePen for an interactive demo.

Bistable Gene Expression

This is a demo of the stochastic gene expression model that I studied during one of my undergraduate research projects at the University of Waterloo. I helped our group by implementing the Gillespie algorithm for this model, using this method to generate a lot of data, and performing some statistical analysis. For more information, please see our conference paper. The input control variable, u, can be adjusted in the simulation to demonstrate bistable switching.
